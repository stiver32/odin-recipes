Easy Bake Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 8 Chicken thighs
  • 2 Tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 Teaspoon paprika
  • 1 Teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 Teaspoon dried parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Lightly coat baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Next, brush olive oil over the chicken thighs. Then combine the seasonings in a small bowl.
  3. Sprinkle the seasoning blend over both sides of the chicken thighs before placing them in the baking dish.
  4. Finally, bake in the oven for 30-40 minutes.

NOTE: internal temperate should read 165 degrees.
